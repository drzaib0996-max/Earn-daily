How to Play

Welcome to LuckyDraw!

Here's how to participate in our exciting lottery draws:

  1. Deposit funds into your account using JazzCash
  2. Buy tickets for the upcoming draw (10 PKR per ticket)
  3. Wait for the draw to happen - we select 2 winners every 24 hours
  4. If you win, the prize money will be added to your account balance
  5. You can withdraw your winnings using JazzCash

Note: The more tickets you buy, the higher your chances of winning!
